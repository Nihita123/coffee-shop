FAQs

What types of coffee do you offer?

We offer a variety of coffee drinks, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, Americano, macchiato, and more. We also have seasonal specialties and a selection of cold brews.

What brewing methods do you recommend?

We recommend various brewing methods depending on your preference, including pour-over, French press, AeroPress, and espresso machines. Each method offers a unique flavor profile and experience.

Can I buy your coffee beans to brew at home?

Absolutely! We sell our freshly roasted coffee beans in-store and online. You can choose from whole beans or have them ground to your preferred brewing method.

Do you offer any classes or workshops on coffee brewing?

Yes, we regularly host coffee brewing workshops and tastings. Check our events page or ask our staff for the latest schedule and to reserve your spot.

What makes your coffee different from others?

Our commitment to quality and sustainability sets us apart. We carefully select and roast our beans to highlight their unique flavors and ensure a fresh, delicious cup of coffee every time.

Where do you source your coffee beans?

Our coffee beans are sourced from premium coffee-growing regions around the world, including South America, Africa, and Asia. We prioritize fair trade and sustainably sourced beans to ensure the highest quality and ethical standards.

Do you offer any dairy-free or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of dairy-free milk alternatives, including almond milk, soy milk, oat milk, and coconut milk. All of our syrups and flavorings are also vegan-friendly.
